RECRUITMENT AND CAREER COUNSELLING/COACHING

Uncover thinking styles as well as strengths and areas for development, highlight personal skills and potential talent.

The Leonardo summary report allows the recruiter to discuss the job requirements in relation to the strengths of the candidate. The evaluation gains in both accuracy and courteousness: the non-judgemental nature, the focusing on strengths and complementarities to try and ensure that the responsibilities and duties of a given position are handled optimally, allows a real negotiation between equal partners.

In the event that a candidate is not selection the Leonardo3.4.5 brief profile, has been designed as a "give away" for the candidate, providing value for applying for the job.

Career counsellors and coaches can equally use the Leonardo3.4.5 detailed report and development path to look at opportunities for professional development and to work through career blocks or tensions at work.
